Learning Academy

Welcome to the Jubilant Learning Academy!

As learners, how great would it be, to be able to access learning modules at the touch of a button, anytime, anywhere? How would you like to revisit the content multiple times? How convenient would it be to learn at your own pace? How about having bite sized learning nuggets available to you that help you to plan for situations at work – it could be to prepare for a meeting, before a crucial conversation, to plan for a sales pitch to a client, to work on a presentation and many more.

The Jubilant Learning Academy brings learning to you and puts you in charge of your professional and personal learning. The Academy provides to you a curated collection of elearning courses across business and Managerial and functional area which will help you supplement your skills. You will also be able to explore the classroom learning catalogue, identify the upcoming sessions and enroll into them. Additionally, you will have access to articles, videos, presentations and other reference material. What’s more? You can carry your classroom in your pocket through mobile learning that is accessible on your smartphone!

So, bring out the curious learner in you and explore this space!
